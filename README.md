## About Me

I am a fourth-year Computer Engineering student at Istanbul Aydın University, focused on building practical solutions in machine learning, data science and artificial intelligence.

My work covers the complete project lifecycle: understanding the problem, preparing data, performing exploratory analysis, developing and evaluating models, exposing predictions through APIs, and packaging applications with Docker.

- Currently working on **machine learning, NLP, RAG and LLM-based applications**
- Interested in **ML Engineering, Data Science and AI Product Development**
- Building reproducible projects with **Python, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow and PyTorch**
- Improving deployment skills with **FastAPI, Docker, Git and MLOps practices**
- Comfortable working with international teams and presenting technical results in English
